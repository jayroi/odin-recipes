Pouring Coca-Cola in a Glass

From Frozen to your Mouth

Listen, if you go down the freezer aisle and eggos are on sale, don't even think. Just buy them. Not only do you get an unhealthy breakfast, you get you daily dose of sugar from all the syrup you are going to put in each of these little squares. This is one of the easiest and fastest ways to get your morning going.

$5 Dollar Breakfast that lasts 5 days

Ingrediants

  • Eggo
  • Toaster
    • g

Steps

  1. Open box
  2. Pull out eggo
  3. Place in toaster/li>
  4. Push toaster lever
  5. Wait 1 minutes
  6. Pour syrup in each square
  7. eat
