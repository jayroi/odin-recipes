Worlds Best Lasagna

Nothign is saucier or cheesier

This is a dish my mother used to make all the time. It truely so delicious and reminds me of the microwaveble ones you get in the frozen food aisle. The main ingrediant is going to be ricotta cheese. Choose whatever brand you want but make sure you get alot, this is going to be the heart of the dish. It is not only for flavor but it gives it the exact texture we are looking for. Another important element is going to be the type of dish you use. I highly recommend a glass dish as a glass dish holds in the perfect amount of heat. Last but not least is the sauce. I always say to make sauce at home but for this dish were goin to with Roas which you can find in most gorcery stores. Honestly just choose your favorite red sauce.

This dish takes about 40 minutes to make, but it really definitely be a hit at your next pot luck or dinner with friends.
